About us

We are a group of college students with a burning passion for music. We do gigs at any events just drop us a call / email !

Est. 2020

Meet the band members:

Monkey D. Luffy

Position: Lead Singer / Leader

Luffy is the leader of the band group the strawhats. The band name is named after his infamous strawhat he wears everyday. His blinding charisma and voice of an angel has earned him the title of fan favourites.

Roronoa Zoro

Position: Bassist

First member to join the band. Zoro is a high school friend of luffy and was convinced to join the band with luffy's persistent persuasion. His looks and skills as a bassist has definitely attracted many female fans.

Blackleg Sanji

Position: Lead Guitarist / Secondary Singer

On top of being the guitarist of the band, Sanji occasionally backs luffy up with amazing harmonisations and even takes the melodies once in a while. He is nicknamed 'mr prince' by his fans as he likes to treat his fangirls as princesses.

Nami

Position: Rhythm Guitarist

Nami may be the only female in the band but it is clear that she is the one in charge. She is in charge of keeping everyone in check during performances as well as normal days. Her baddass energy has gained a lot of attention from both fangirls and fanboys. She also doubles as a manager for the band and is in charge of all the expenses of the band.

Ussop

Position: Drummer

Ussop brings sick beats to all the shows with his incredible skills as a drummer. During intermissions he brings many laughter to the crowd with his stories dubbed 'the incredible tales of the great captain Ussop'. He is also to known to always get cold feet before gigs and shows but never disappoints the crowd with his drumming skills.
